## 3. NATURE OF ZERO HOURS ARRANGEMENT

This is a zero-hours contract. The Employer is not obliged to offer you any minimum number of hours of work in any given week, and you are not obliged to accept any offer of work made to you.

Where work is offered and accepted, the terms of this contract will apply during the period of work. You are a worker and not an independent contractor.

This arrangement does not prevent you from working for other employers. However, if you accept work with a third party that could conflict with your duties at Little Angels Day Nursery (for example, another OFSTED-registered childcare provider), you must notify the Employer in advance.

- Supporting learning activities in line with the EYFS framework
- Maintaining a safe, clean, and nurturing environment
- Communicating professionally with children, families, and colleagues
- Following all safeguarding and data protection policies
- Carrying out any other reasonable duties requested by the manager

## 8. ANNUAL LEAVE

You accrue paid annual leave at the statutory rate of 5.6 weeks per year (pro rata to hours worked). For zero-hours workers, holiday pay is calculated as 12.07% of hours worked in each pay period and paid as a separate element on your payslip (rolled-up holiday pay), unless an alternative arrangement is agreed in writing.
