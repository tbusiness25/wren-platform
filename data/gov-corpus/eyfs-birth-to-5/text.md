## Communication and Language

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_01a]** Turns head or quietens towards a familiar voice or sound

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_01b]** Coos and babbles, repeating sounds like 'ba-ba' or 'ga-ga' back and forth with an adult

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_01c]** Settles or shows pleasure when sung a familiar song or rhyme

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_01a]** Responds to own name by turning or looking

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_01b]** Follows a simple request paired with a gesture, such as 'wave bye-bye'

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_01c]** Joins in with actions or sounds during a familiar song or rhyme

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_01a]** Points at an object or picture to share interest or ask for it

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_01b]** Uses several recognisable single words to name people or things

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_01c]** Holds a book the right way up and turns the pages while looking at it

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_01a]** Puts two words together to make a short phrase, such as 'more juice'

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_01b]** Follows a simple instruction with two parts, such as 'get your cup and sit down'

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_01c]** Listens to a short story and shows interest by looking at the pictures

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_01a]** Uses sentences of four or more words to describe what is happening

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_01b]** Asks 'why', 'who' or 'what' questions to find out more

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_01c]** Joins in with the repeated refrain of a familiar story and anticipates what comes next

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_01a]** Retells a simple past event or recent experience in the correct order

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_01b]** Listens to and follows a multi-step instruction without needing it repeated

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_01c]** Holds a back-and-forth conversation, staying on topic and taking turns to respond


## Personal, Social and Emotional Development

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_02a]** Calms or settles when picked up and held by a familiar adult.

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_02b]** Looks intently at a carer's face and holds eye contact during a cuddle or feed.

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_02c]** Smiles back at a familiar person who smiles and talks to them.

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_02a]** Crawls or moves back to a trusted adult to 'check in' before exploring again.

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_02b]** Shows pleasure in a familiar game such as peek-a-boo by laughing or wanting it repeated.

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_02c]** Becomes wary of, or watches closely, a stranger before being willing to engage.

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_02a]** Plays happily alongside another child without yet joining in their play.

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_02b]** Brings or points out an object to share their interest with an adult.

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_02c]** Pushes back against help, wanting to 'do it myself' such as feeding or putting on a shoe.

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_02a]** Names or points to themselves in a mirror or photo, showing awareness of being 'me'.

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_02b]** Uses a comfort object or seeks a key adult to soothe themselves when upset.

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_02c]** Begins to take turns with help, such as rolling a ball back and forth with an adult.

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_02a]** Initiates play with another child and keeps a shared game going for a short time.

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_02b]** Names how they are feeling, such as saying they are sad, cross or happy.

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_02c]** Confidently chooses an activity and tells an adult what they want to do.

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_02a]** Waits for a turn and follows the rules of a simple game with other children.

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_02b]** Suggests a way to solve a disagreement, such as offering to share or take turns.

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_02c]** Talks about how a friend might be feeling and offers comfort or help.


## Physical Development

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_03a]** Turns head towards a sound or interesting sight and follows it with the eyes

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_03b]** Lifts and holds the head steady when placed on their tummy

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_03c]** Reaches out for a nearby toy and brings it to the mouth to explore

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_03a]** Sits without support and reaches for a toy without toppling over

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_03b]** Moves across the floor by crawling, shuffling or rolling to reach something

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_03c]** Picks up a small object such as a raisin between thumb and finger

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_03a]** Walks steadily across the room without holding on to furniture

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_03b]** Sits down on and gets back up from a low chair without help

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_03c]** Holds a crayon in a fist and makes marks on paper

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_03a]** Kicks a stationary ball forwards with one foot

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_03b]** Climbs up and down a step or low climbing frame using both hands and feet

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_03c]** Turns the pages of a book one at a time and uses a spoon to feed themselves

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_03a]** Runs around obstacles and stops or changes direction without bumping into things

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_03b]** Pedals and steers a tricycle or ride-on toy around a space

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_03c]** Uses scissors to make snips along the edge of paper

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_03a]** Hops on one foot and balances on one leg for a few seconds

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_03b]** Catches a large ball thrown towards them using both hands

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_03c]** Holds a pencil with a tripod grip and forms recognisable letters or shapes


## Literacy

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_04a]** Turns head or stills to listen when an adult sings a familiar song or rhyme nearby.

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_04b]** Looks at a brightly patterned board book held in front of them and reaches out to pat or grasp it.

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_04c]** Babbles or coos back when an adult shares a picture book and talks about what they can see.

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_04a]** Brings a chosen book to a familiar adult to share.

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_04b]** Pats or points at a picture in a book when the adult names it.

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_04c]** Joins in with actions or sounds during a familiar action rhyme or song.

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_04a]** Holds a book the right way up and turns the pages, often several at a time.

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_04b]** Says or fills in a familiar word or sound when an adult pauses in a well-known rhyme.

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_04c]** Makes marks on paper using a crayon or chunky pen in a fisted grip.

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_04a]** Chooses to look at books independently in the book area for a short time.

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_04b]** Notices and points to print or pictures in the room, such as a sign or a label.

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_04c]** Gives meaning to their own marks, saying what a scribble or drawing represents.

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_04a]** Joins in with repeated refrains in a story and anticipates what comes next.

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_04b]** Hears and claps the separate syllables in a spoken word, such as their own name.

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_04c]** Recognises the first letter or sound of their own name when shown it.

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_04a]** Blends the spoken sounds in a simple word together to say the whole word, such as c-a-t.

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_04b]** Writes some recognisable letters, including those from their own name.

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_04c]** Retells a familiar story in sequence using the language patterns of the book.


## Mathematics

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_05a]** Stares longer at a tray when the number of objects on it suddenly changes.

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_05b]** Reaches towards the plate holding the larger amount of food or more toys.

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_05c]** Follows a slowly moving object with their eyes as it travels across their view.

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_05a]** Repeatedly fills a container with objects and tips them all out again.

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_05b]** Posts shapes or objects into matching holes or gaps.

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_05c]** Pushes away a small amount and gestures or vocalises for 'more'.

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_05a]** Stacks two or three blocks on top of one another to build upwards.

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_05b]** Joins in with number words or actions during a counting song or rhyme.

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_05c]** Squeezes into a box, tunnel or other small space to explore how their body fits.

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_05a]** Says some number names in sequence while playing, even if not yet in order.

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_05b]** Sorts a pile of objects into groups by colour, size or type.

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_05c]** Completes an inset puzzle by turning and fitting each piece into its space.

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_05a]** Counts a small set of objects by touching each one in turn.

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_05b]** Names a circle, square or triangle when shown the shape.

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_05c]** Lines up two sets of objects to check which group has more.

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_05a]** Counts a row of objects and says the total without recounting.

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_05b]** Shares a set of objects out equally between two or more people.

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_05c]** Continues a simple repeating pattern, such as red-blue-red-blue, with objects.


## Understanding the World

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_06a]** Turns head or looks towards a sound, light or moving object that catches their attention

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_06b]** Brings objects to the mouth and explores them by feeling, mouthing and looking closely

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_06c]** Shows interest in the face of a familiar carer, watching and responding to them

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_06a]** Repeats an action to make an interesting effect happen again, such as banging or shaking a toy

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_06b]** Looks for a partly hidden object or a toy that has rolled out of sight

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_06c]** Explores how things work by pressing, opening, posting or pulling parts of a toy

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_06a]** Notices and reacts to changes around them, such as the weather, lights going on, or a familiar pet

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_06b]** Operates simple equipment, for example pushing a button to start a toy or turning a knob

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_06c]** Explores natural materials like water, sand, soil or leaves using their hands and senses

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_06a]** Shows curiosity about living things, pointing to or naming animals, insects or plants they see

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_06b]** Talks about people who are special to them, such as family members or a key person

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_06c]** Joins in with simple everyday routines that mark the passing of the day, like tidy-up or snack time

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_06a]** Comments on changes they notice in the natural world, such as ice melting or plants growing

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_06b]** Talks about a recent event or experience from their own life in the right order

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_06c]** Shows awareness that other families and friends may do things differently from their own

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_06a]** Describes a simple change or life cycle they have observed, such as a caterpillar becoming a butterfly

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_06b]** Talks about similarities and differences between their own life and the lives of other people

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_06c]** Uses a piece of technology independently to complete a chosen task, such as taking a photo or playing a game


## Expressive Arts and Design

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_07a]** Turns towards or stills to a familiar sung voice or simple melody.

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_07b]** Watches a carer's face and movements, gazing intently at expressions during play.

**Range 1 (Birth to 12 months)**: **[B25M_R1_07c]** Explores a sound-making object such as a rattle, repeating an action to hear the noise again.

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_07a]** Bounces, sways or moves the body in response to a lively rhythm or song.

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_07b]** Bangs, taps or shakes objects to deliberately make a sound.

**Range 2 (8 to 20 months)**: **[B25M_R2_07c]** Makes marks by patting or smearing a textured material such as paint or food.

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_07a]** Joins in with actions or sounds during a familiar song or rhyme.

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_07b]** Makes random marks on paper with a crayon or chunky pen held in the fist.

**Range 3 (16 to 26 months)**: **[B25M_R3_07c]** Shows interest by exploring different textures and materials with the hands.

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_07a]** Uses a single toy or block to stand for something else in simple pretend play.

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_07b]** Sings or hums fragments of a familiar song, matching some of the words or tune.

**Range 4 (22 to 36 months)**: **[B25M_R4_07c]** Experiments with combining colours or materials, watching what happens as they mix.

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_07a]** Acts out a short remembered experience, taking on a role such as feeding a doll.

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_07b]** Chooses tools and materials on purpose to make a representation of an idea or object.

**Range 5 (30 to 50 months)**: **[B25M_R5_07c]** Moves the body expressively, matching speed or mood to the music being played.

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_07a]** Plans and builds a model or picture, explaining what it represents to others.

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_07b]** Invents and develops a storyline in role play, agreeing roles with other children.

**Range 6 (40 to 60+ months)**: **[B25M_R6_07c]** Performs a song, dance or rhyme to others, keeping in time with a steady beat.
