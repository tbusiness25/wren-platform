How our spaces work

  • Baby Room (from 6 months): full days only — it's a small room, so we keep it full-time.
  • Pre-school (2–5 years): part-time is possible; we usually ask for a minimum of 3 days.
  • Leaving & notice: families give around 6 weeks' notice, so spaces often open at short notice — it's always worth being on the list.
  • The waiting list: we keep it short and honest. When a place opens we offer it in turn, with a short window to confirm, so nobody is left in limbo.
