🔬 External Verification

These tests verify your setup from outside your network. None of them involve any Wren infrastructure — they contact only your own server and public third-party services.

A — Test via SSL Labs
Opens Qualys SSL Labs in a new tab to test your TLS configuration. Free, no account needed. Gets you an A–F grade on your HTTPS security.
B — Test from your phone
Generates a one-time QR code. Scan it on your phone using mobile data (not nursery wifi) — this makes the test come from a genuinely external network. Your phone runs in-browser checks and posts results back to your server only.

The results page is served from your own server. Wren never sees them.
C — Run from another machine
Run this command from any machine on a different network (e.g. your home laptop). The open-source scanner will be published at the GitHub URL below.
