How our waiting list works

Your Nursery · Ealing

We'd rather be honest with you than give false hope. We're a small, two-room nursery, so spaces are genuinely limited and they move month to month. This page explains — plainly — how we take children on, so you know exactly where you stand.

Our two rooms

Full-time only

Baby Room

From 6 months to around 2 years

  • It's a small room with very few places, so we keep it full-time — that's what keeps it viable and staffed to ratio.
  • We usually can't take a baby much past around 18 months into this room — there wouldn't be enough time for them to settle before they move up to Pre-school.
Part-time possible

Pre-school

2 to 5 years

  • More flexible — part-time is possible, though we usually ask for a minimum of 3 days so children settle into a routine and the days stay balanced.
  • Children join here from around age 2. We don't take children who are too young for the room, as they settle far better once they're ready.

The things that surprise people

  • First come, first served. When a space opens, we work through the list in order. Being on the list early genuinely matters.
  • Baby Room is full-time only. We know that's not what every family wants, but with so few baby places we can only make it work by keeping them full — otherwise the room isn't sustainable.
  • Dropping days isn't always possible. If you're with us and want to go from, say, 5 days to 4, we'd love to help — but a spare full day has to actually be free on the days you'd drop. Sometimes it is; sometimes it takes a little while.
  • Spaces often open at short notice. Families typically give us around 6 weeks' notice, and most "school leavers" are actually nursery-school leavers who also only give 6 weeks — so a place can appear sooner than you'd expect. It's always worth being on the list.
  • We keep the list short and honest. When a place opens we offer it in turn with a short window to confirm, so nobody is left waiting in limbo for a decision that's already been made.
💡 Not sure there's a space right now? You can ask us to keep you on the waiting list. We'll hold your details on file and get in touch the moment something opens on the days you need — no chasing required.
