Wren EY App Help
Quick reference for practitioners at Little Angels Day Nursery.
Getting Started
📱 Logging In ›

Enter your 4-digit PIN on the login screen. Sessions last 15 minutes of inactivity — you'll see a warning banner before you're signed out.

If you forget your PIN, ask the manager (Toby) to reset it from the admin portal.

🏠 The Home Screen ›

The home screen shows all active children as a grid. Use the filter chips (Baby Room / Pre-school) to narrow the view, or tap the search icon to find a child by name.

Long-press (hold 0.5s) on any child card to enter multi-select mode, then tap additional children for a group action.

Observations
📝 Writing an Observation ›
  1. Tap a child's card on the home screen.
  2. Tap Add Observation (or use the + button in the bottom nav).
  3. Type your observation text — drafts save automatically every 2 seconds.
  4. Add EYFS statements, a photo, or a voice note if needed.
  5. Tap Save — the observation is linked to the child's learning journey.

Observations shared with parents appear in the parents portal immediately after saving.

👥 Group Observations ›

Select multiple children (long-press then tap), then tap Group Actions → Group Observation. The same text and photos are linked to all selected children at once.

📄 Saved Drafts ›

If you start an observation but don't save it, the text is kept in a local draft. Find it under More → Drafts. Tap Continue to pick up where you left off.

Drafts are stored on this device only — they won't appear on another tablet.

Daily Records
🍽️ Food, Sleep & Nappies ›

Use the + button → Group Sleep Check / Group Food (etc.) to log group records quickly. Individual child records can be added from the child's profile page.

Parents can see sleep and food records in their portal diary view.

💊 Medicine Records ›

Medicine records require manager sign-off. Go to the child's profile, tap Medicine, complete the form, then ask Toby to authorise it from the admin portal.

Never administer medicine without a completed and signed record.

Trackers & Reports
📊 Framework Trackers ›

The Trackers page shows EYFS area coverage (which statements have been linked to observations), phonics sound progress, and first words logged for the Baby Room.

Coverage increases when you link EYFS statements to observations in the observation form.
