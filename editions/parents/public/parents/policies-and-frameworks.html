Policies & Frameworks

These are the laws and statutory frameworks that Little Angels Day Nursery follows to keep your child safe, well-supported, and learning. You can read any of them in full.

Why share these? As a parent, you have a right to know the legal frameworks your child's nursery is required to follow. Every document listed here is published by the UK government and is freely available to the public under the Open Government Licence. We've gathered them in one place for your convenience.
