    Phonics scheme

    Which validated systematic synthetic phonics (SSP) programme does your setting follow? You can change this any time.

    Staff

    Add the people who'll log in. You need at least one manager — that's who logs in after setup. You can import a spreadsheet, add people by hand, or skip and add them later.

    Columns: first_name, last_name, email, role, pin. Roles: manager, deputy_manager, practitioner, teacher, ta.

    ⬇ Download sample staff CSV

    Rooms & classes

    Add your rooms or classes. Quick-add below, or skip for now.

    Columns: name, capacity, year_group.

    Children & pupils

    Import a simple pupil list to get started, or skip. Richer imports (Tapestry, CTF, BrightHR) are available after login in System → Import Wizard.

    Columns: first_name, last_name, date_of_birth, year_group (date as YYYY-MM-DD).

    ⬇ Download sample pupils CSV

    Optional modules

    Turn on the modules you want. Safeguarding, incidents and medicine are always on. You can change these any time in System.
